## Source Credibility Hierarchy

| Priority | Source Type | Examples | Use For |
|----------|-------------|----------|---------|
| 1 | Industry Reports | Gartner, Forrester, IDC, McKinsey | TAM/SAM/SOM, market trends |
| 2 | Government/Official | Census, BLS, SEC filings, Trade associations | Market size validation |
| 3 | Academic Research | Google Scholar, ResearchGate | Long-term trends, methodology |
| 4 | Company Data | Annual reports, investor presentations | Competitor financials |
| 5 | Review Platforms | G2, Capterra, TrustRadius | Feature comparison, sentiment |
| 6 | News/Press | TechCrunch, VentureBeat, company blogs | Recent announcements |
| 7 | Community | Reddit, HackerNews, StackOverflow | User sentiment, pain points |

## Sources by Analysis Type

### Competitor Analysis
- **G2.com** - Feature comparison, user reviews, market grid
- **Capterra** - Software comparison, pricing, reviews
- **Crunchbase** - Funding, team size, company info
- **SimilarWeb** - Traffic estimates, audience overlap
- **BuiltWith** - Technology stack detection
- **LinkedIn** - Company size, hiring trends, job postings

### Market Sizing (TAM/SAM/SOM)
- **Statista** - Market size reports, forecasts
- **IBISWorld** - Industry reports, market analysis
- **Grand View Research** - Market size, growth projections
- **Markets and Markets** - Technology market reports
- **Gartner** - Magic Quadrant, market guides
- **SEC EDGAR** - Public company filings (10-K, 10-Q)

### Trend Analysis
- **Google Trends** - Search interest over time
- **GitHub Trending** - Technology adoption signals
- **StackOverflow Survey** - Developer trends
- **State of JS/CSS/etc.** - Annual technology surveys
- **ThoughtWorks Radar** - Technology assessment
- **Hype Cycle (Gartner)** - Technology maturity

### Customer Profile (JTBD)
- **UserTesting** - Customer interviews, usability
- **Hotjar** - User behavior analytics
- **ProductHunt** - Early adopter feedback
- **Reddit/Forums** - Pain points, feature requests
- **Support tickets** - Common issues, needs
- **NPS surveys** - Customer satisfaction data

### Ocean Analysis
- **Porter's Five Forces data** - Industry structure
- **Patent databases** - Innovation activity
- **VC funding trends** - Market interest signals
- **Job postings analysis** - Growth indicators
- **Regulatory filings** - Market entry barriers

## Data Freshness Requirements

| Data Type | Max Age | Refresh Frequency |
|-----------|---------|-------------------|
| Market size | 12 months | Annual |
| Competitor features | 3 months | Quarterly |
| Pricing | 1 month | Monthly |
| Trends | 6 months | Bi-annual |
| Customer sentiment | 3 months | Quarterly |

## Quality Checklist

- [ ] Source date within acceptable range
- [ ] Cross-referenced with 2+ sources
- [ ] Methodology documented (for estimates)
- [ ] Geographic scope specified
- [ ] Currency and units consistent
- [ ] Confidence level noted (High/Medium/Low)
